# CID Connect — Marketing Outline (Features & Benefits)

Use this doc as the **source brief** for Claude (or any creative) to write landing copy, ads, email sequences, social posts, and sales one-pagers. Focus on **outcomes for the business owner**, not internal architecture.

---

## Positioning (one sentence)

**CID Connect is the insured’s command center** — policy, certificates, coverage answers, claims, and renewals in one mobile-friendly app, built for commercial businesses (bars, roofers, plumbers, HVAC, fitness, and more).

---

## Primary audience

- **Who:** Owner-operators and office managers at small-to-mid commercial businesses with active CID policies.
- **Pain:** Scattered email threads for COIs, unclear coverage when something happens, paper policies in a drawer, renewal surprises, slow answers from traditional agents.
- **Desire:** “Handle insurance like I handle the rest of my business — on my phone, fast, with a clear record.”

---

## Brand voice cues

- Confident, plain English, no jargon unless explained.
- **Partner, not gatekeeper** — we help them self-serve; human support is backup.
- **Segment-aware** — speak their trade (liquor liability for bars, torch work for roofers, etc.).
- Mobile-first, professional, trustworthy (navy + orange accent palette).

---

## Core value pillars

| Pillar | Promise |
|--------|---------|
| **Clarity** | See your policy, limits, and carrier in one place. |
| **Speed** | Request and track COIs without phone tag. |
| **Confidence** | Ask “Am I covered?” before a loss, not after. |
| **Continuity** | Quotes, bind, documents, and renewals stay connected to the same account. |

## Feature → Benefit map (full product)

### 1. Policy Vault (home)

**Features**
- Active policy card: number, business name, segment, carrier, premium, effective/expiration dates.
- Renewal banner when expiration is within 60 days.
- Quick actions to COI, coverage chat, documents, claims, billing, timeline.
- Carrier detail drill-down.

**Benefits**
- “Open the app and know you’re covered — no digging through email.”
- “Renewal won’t sneak up on you.”
- “One tap to the things you actually need week to week.”

**Headline angles**
- Your policy, not a PDF in a folder.
- Insurance that fits in your pocket.

---

### 2. COI Hub — Instant COI, tracking & certificate holder book *(Phase 1 highlight)*

**Features**
- Request a Certificate of Insurance from the app (holder name, address, delivery email, certificate type, optional requirements upload).
- **Status pipeline:** Received → Generating → Sent.
- **Active requests** on the COI home tab — see what’s in progress without calling anyone.
- **Track & History:** full list with filters, download link when complete, who/what/when on each request.
- **Certificate Holder Book:** saved holders from past requests; **Reissue** pre-fills the form for repeat landlords, GCs, and vendors.
- Async fulfillment: PDF generated and delivered via email when ready.

**Benefits**
- “Landlord needs a COI by 5pm — submit in two minutes, track until it’s sent.”
- “Stop retyping the same GC address every month.”
- “Proof of insurance without playing phone tag with your agent.”

**Headline angles**
- COIs on your schedule, not theirs.
- Your certificate holder book, always ready to reissue.
- From request to inbox — tracked every step.

**Objection handlers**
- *“My agent always did this.”* → Same outcome, faster, with a record you control.
- *“What if they need special wording?”* → Upload requirements; choose waiver / primary non-contributory options.

---

### 3. Am I Covered? — AI coverage assistant *(Phase 1 highlight)*

**Features**
- **Segment-specific scenario buttons** (e.g., liquor liability & assault for bars; worker fall & torch work for roofers; water damage & gas line for plumbers).
- Free-form chat about limits, exclusions, deductibles.
- Policy-aware when bridge/API context is available.
- Claude primary, Gemini fallback (reliable answers).

**Benefits**
- “Know before you say yes to the event, the job, or the lease requirement.”
- “Questions in plain English — answers without waiting on hold.”
- “Scenarios built for *your* trade, not generic small-business fluff.”

**Headline angles**
- Ask before it’s a claim.
- Coverage clarity in 30 seconds.
- Built for bars. Built for roofers. Built for you.

**Sample scenario hooks (by segment)**
- **Bar:** “Am I covered if a fight breaks out on my premises?”
- **Roofer:** “Am I covered if a roof we installed leaks?”
- **Plumber:** “Am I covered if a job causes water damage?”
- **HVAC:** “Am I covered for a refrigerant leak claim?”

---

### 4. Policy Timeline

**Features**
- Chronological story: quote, bind, policy issued, COI events, claims.
- Rich COI entries: holder, delivery email, type, status, completion time.

**Benefits**
- “See the full history when someone asks ‘when did we send that COI?’”
- “One narrative for audits, landlords, and your own records.”

**Headline angles**
- Your insurance story, one timeline.
- Who got what, and when.

---

### 5. Quotes & bind

**Features**
- Segment quote entry (routes to segment intake where configured).
- Quote history and comparison.
- Bind-token onboarding: link from bind email → account → policy in app.

**Benefits**
- “From quote to policy without starting over.”
- “Compare options before you commit.”

**Headline angles**
- Quote today. Manage tomorrow — in the same app.

---

### 6. Claims

**Features**
- File a claim from the app (incident details, photos).
- Claim history and status tracking.

**Benefits**
- “Start the claim the moment something happens.”
- “Photos and details captured while it’s fresh.”

**Headline angles**
- When something goes wrong, you’re not starting from zero.

---

### 7. Documents & downloads

**Features**
- Policy documents, declarations, generated COI PDFs (when available).
- Download/share from the app.

**Benefits**
- “Dec page for the bank, COI for the GC — without inbox archaeology.”

---

### 8. Billing & payment

**Features**
- View billing context; update payment method (where enabled).

**Benefits**
- “Keep coverage active — update card before a lapse.”

---

### 9. Renewals

**Features**
- Renewal reminders and comparison shopping entry.
- Renewal banner on policy home.

**Benefits**
- “No surprise lapses; shop renewal before expiration.”

**Headline angles**
- Renew on your terms, not in a panic.

---

### 10. Carrier browser & detail

**Features**
- Browse carriers; view carrier-specific info tied to your policy.

**Benefits**
- “Know who stands behind your policy.”

---

### 11. Profile & PWA

**Features**
- Install CID Connect to home screen (PWA).
- Account, documents shortcut, help email, privacy link.

**Benefits**
- “Works like an app — no App Store required.”
- “Always one tap away on the job site or behind the bar.”

**Headline angles**
- Add to Home Screen. Run your insurance like an app.

## Compliance / disclaimer snippets (include in footers)

- Coverage chat provides **general guidance** based on your policy information; it is **not legal advice** and does not guarantee claim payment. Confirm material decisions with your carrier or licensed representative.
- COI delivery times depend on verification and generation; urgent requests may still need direct support.
